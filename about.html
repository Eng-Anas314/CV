Translating Algorithms into Physical Motion

I am a Mechatronics Engineer specializing in the intersection of mechanical design, hardware prototyping, and embedded intelligence.

Mechanical Design

Precision modeling, 3D printing, and resin casting using SolidWorks and Blender.

Electronics & PCB

Circuit design, schematic capture, and PCB routing using KiCad.

Embedded Software

Real-time PID control loops and edge processing using C++, Python, and Arduino/ESP32.

The Digital Twin Approach

Before physical construction, systems are simulated in MATLAB/Simscape to validate mechanical kinematics and control logic, reducing iteration time on physical prototypes.

Edge AI & Control Systems

Integrating high-level processing, such as smartphone-based computer vision, with low-level ESP32 hardware to achieve stable, automated physical responses in robotics and hexacopter builds.

Current Focus

Developing a comprehensive "Phone-Brain" system interface, bridging Dart/Flutter and Python via FastAPI and USB communication protocols for advanced mobile-hardware integration.
